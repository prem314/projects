Help me write a puzzle book on identifying evil. The format of the book will be as follows. There will multiple puzzles, each containing some people's profile.

  - First, there will be neutral profile on some person. Starting with one profile picture where the person is in a neutral setting. Give the person's name, basic neutral details and more pictures if available. Then, ask the user: Is this person evil or not?
  - After the reader has answered this question on their own, from the next page will be the reveal. Either the person will be an obscure person having lead a normal public life. Or there will be a dark history to the person. In either case, give details on the person.
  - The idea of the book is this: looks is deceptive. A person who looks normal, may not actually be normal. People hide dark secrets, and it is dangerous to assume that the other person is just like you.

The entire book will have roughly 50% normal people and 50% people with known dark secret. This will be the entire structure of the book.

Further instructions:
  - Build the book in tex. Each person-profile will have his own tex file, and collect all the tex files together.
  - The folder 'research' contains some rough research notes for the book, like the list of the people to include.
  - The folder 'book_pipeline' contains scripts and helper code for fetching person metadata, reusable images, license/source evidence, and structured inputs for the TeX files.
  - The person who has no public record of evil should not be a very popular person, or people will know immediately that the guy is innocent.
